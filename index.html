Growing naturally.

At Coastside Microgreens we use the highest quaulity organic gardening soil without pesticide or adding fertilizers. We harvest on a specific day to optimize flavor exerience and nutrition benefit. Our growing enviorment is indoor so we can regulate and control temperatures (@73°F) while eliminating risk of insects, mold, or fungus damage.
