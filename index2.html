Microgreens benefit hair & skin.

Sunflower, radish, broccoli, kale, and nasturtium microgreens provide essential skin nutrients such as zinc, vitamins A, B, D, E, sulforaphane antioxidants like polyphenols. Microgreens also have anti-inflammatory effects which can be credited to the antioxidants existing within these tiny but mighty plants.
